Paying for your ticket in person

The Parking Citation Assistance Center is located at 250 Frank H. Ogawa Plaza, Suite 6300, near Oakland City Hall. It is open Mondays, Tuesdays, Thursdays and Fridays from 9 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. and Wednesdays from 9:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. The phone number is (800) 500-6484.

For your convenience, parking ticket payment kiosks (photo at right) are located at: Dalziel Building lobby, 250 Frank H. Ogawa Plaza, Monday through Friday from 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. Eastmont Police Substation, 2651 73rd Avenue, Monday through Saturday from 8:00 a.m. to 4 p.m.
